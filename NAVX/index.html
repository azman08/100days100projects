01 About

What is NAVX?

A sticky navbar is one of the most common UI patterns — and one of the most poorly implemented. Most developers just slap position: sticky and call it done.

NAVX is a fully engineered navbar system with 8 distinct scroll behaviors, active section tracking, mobile drawer, scroll progress indicator, and a live state debugger — all in pure HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

No frameworks. No scroll libraries. Just clean, precise frontend engineering.

02 Features

8 Scroll Behaviors

01

Sticky on Scroll

Navbar locks to top after scrolling past the hero. Uses IntersectionObserver for zero-overhead detection.

02

Hide on Scroll Down

Navbar slides up and hides when scrolling down, giving full screen space for content.

03

Reveal on Scroll Up

Instantly slides back down the moment you scroll up — smooth CSS transform transition.

04

Glassmorphism Effect

Background switches from transparent to frosted glass with backdrop-filter: blur() after scrolling.

05

Active Section Tracking

IntersectionObserver watches all sections and highlights the correct nav link in real time.

06

Scroll Progress Bar

A thin progress bar at the top of the navbar fills as you scroll through the page.

07

Shrink on Scroll

Navbar height and font size reduce slightly after scrolling — creating a compact, focused state.

08

Mobile Drawer

Animated hamburger → close icon morphing. Full-screen mobile menu with staggered link animations.
